1. Introduction

Welcome to GhostSolution's Cookie Policy page. We want to inform you that our website does not use cookies to collect or store personal information.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Our Cookie-Free Approach

At GhostSolution, we have chosen not to use cookies or similar tracking technologies on our website. This means:

  • We do not track your activity across our website or other websites
  • We do not store information about your preferences or your device
  • We do not use any tracking pixels, web beacons, local storage, or other similar technologies to collect information about you
  • Your browsing experience on our site is completely private

4. Website Functionality

Our site is designed to function without the need for cookies. All features, including the interactive terminal and visual effects, are executed locally in your browser without storing persistent information.

Preferences you set (such as enabling/disabling sound) are not saved between browsing sessions.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating this page.

We encourage you to periodically review this page to stay informed about our approach regarding cookies.
